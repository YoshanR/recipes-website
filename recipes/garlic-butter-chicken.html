Crispy Garlic Butter Chicken

Home

Description

  • My easy garlic butter chicken recipe is a weeknight favorite!
  • Make juicy chicken breast with butter, garlic, and herbs in just 20 minutes.

    Ingredients

    • Potatoes
    • Cooking spray oil
    • Paprika
    • Garlic powder
    • Onion powder
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Parsley
    • Chicken breast
    • Plain flour
    • Corn flour
    • Water
    • Butter
    • Garlic
    • Soy sauce

    Steps

    1. Dice potatoes and season
    2. Bake or air fry for 30=35 mins at 200 degrees celcius
    3. Dice chicken and season
    4. Add plain flour, corn flour and water
    5. Bake or air fry for 15 mins at 200 degrees celcius
    6. On a medium pan melt butter
    7. Add diced garlic
    8. Stir
    9. Add a splash of soy sauce
    10. Add parsley
    11. Add chicken and coat with sauce
    12. Mix potatoes

    And there you have it, easy to make, crispy garlic butter chicken
