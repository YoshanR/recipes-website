Pasta Salad

Home

Description

  • Pasta salad, known in Italian as insalata di pasta or pasta fredda, is a dish prepared with one or more types of pasta, almost always chilled or room temperature, and most often tossed in a vinegar, oil or mayonnaise-based dressing.
  • It is typically served as an appetiser or first course.

    • Ingredients

    • Pasta (Preferrebly penne)
    • Cucumber
    • Tomatoes
    • Lettuce
    • Olives
    • Carrots
    • Mayonnaise
    • Cheese

    Steps

    1. Cook pasta according to instructions of packaging
    2. Wash vegetables
    3. Chop vegetables to your preferred size
    4. Add to large bowl
    5. Once pasta is cooked, strain and leave to cool
    6. Grate cheese then add to bowl
    7. Mix
    8. Once pasta is cool, add to bowl
    9. Mix
    10. Add Mayo, salt and pepper
    11. Mix
    12. To preserve, leave in fridge

    And there you have it, a simple, easy to make meal. Enjoy!
